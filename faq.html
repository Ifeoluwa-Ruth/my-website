Here are some Frequently Asked Questions and Answers:

Q: What flavours do we offer for our cakes?
A: We offer a wide range of flavours including classic vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, lemon and various specialty flavours. Check our menu for the full selection.

Q: Can I place a custom cake order for a special occasion?
A: Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings and other events.
Contact us in advance to discuss your design and flavour preferences.

Q: Do you accomodate dietary restrictions like gluten-free orvegan?
A: Yes, we have options for gluten-free and vegan cakes. Please inform us about any dietary restrictions when placing your order.

Q: How far in advance should I place my order?
A: Yes, online ordering is avalible. Our delivery radius is within 100km. Additional fees may apply based on the delivery distance.

Q: What sizes do your cakes come in how many people do they typically serve?
A: Our cakes come in various sizes to suit your needs, from small to large. Serving sizes depend on the type of cake and range from X to Y servings.

A: Yes, we offer wedding cake tasting by appointment. Contact us to schedule a tasing session and discuss your wedding cake design.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations made within 24hours of the scheduled pickup or delivery time may be subject to a fee. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.

Q: Do you offer cake decorating classes or workshops?
A: Currently, we don't offer regular classes, but we occasionally host special decorating workshop. Follow our social media or newsletter for updates on upcoming events.

Q: How should I store my leftover cakes?
A: To maintain freshness, store your leftover cake in an airtight container in the refrigerator. Bring it to room temperature before serving for best taste.
